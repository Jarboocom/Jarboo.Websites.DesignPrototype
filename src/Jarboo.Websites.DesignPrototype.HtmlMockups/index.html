Employees, consultants and outsourcing has pro and cons.

Employees

are great for building loyal teams with domain knowledge, but they're not scaleable and difficult to hire (and very risky).

Consultants

are technical domain experts and scaleable, but very expensive and not loyal.

Outsourcing
